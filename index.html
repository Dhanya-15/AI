How Artificial Intelligence is Transforming Education

Artificial Intelligence (AI) — the simulation of human intelligence in machines is rapidly reshaping how we learn and teach. In education, AI is transforming classrooms into dynamic, data-driven ecosystems — personalizing lessons, automating routine tasks, and expanding access to quality learning. Yet, it also challenges us to rethink ethics, equity, and the role of human connection in the digital age.
