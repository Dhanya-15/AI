AI in Education Blog

Digital Inclusion

  • Subsidized devices and public-private connectivity programs to guarantee universal access.

  • Community Learning Hubs – Local centers with devices and internet access for students lacking home resources.

AI Transparency & Data Rights

  • Vendors must disclose model behavior and schools should obtain informed consent for data use.

  • Explainable AI Models – Ensure AI decisions like grading or recommendations are interpretable.

Teacher Training

  • Continuous professional development to equip teachers to use AI tools responsibly and effectively.

  • Hands-on Workshops – Practical sessions to integrate AI tools into lesson planning and classroom management.
